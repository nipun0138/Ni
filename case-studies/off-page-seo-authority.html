Client Profile

A mid-size digital agency with good content but weak backlink profile. Had DA (Domain Authority) of 18, struggling to rank for competitive keywords despite quality content. Wanted to build institutional authority to outrank competitors.

Business Type: Digital Agency | Starting DA: 18 | Competitors: DA 45-55

The Challenge

Without strong backlinks, their site couldn't compete in competitive niches regardless of content quality. They had limited understanding of how to earn links strategically and no off-page SEO plan.

  • Low domain authority relative to competitors
  • Minimal backlink profile (only 15 referring domains)
  • No systematic link earning strategy
  • Unclear which types of links would be most valuable
  • Limited resources for outreach

Off-Page SEO Strategy

Linkable Content Creation
We created original research, industry reports, and case studies specifically designed to earn backlinks. These became natural link magnets.

Strategic Outreach
We identified 200+ high-authority sites in their niche and conducted targeted outreach with their most linkable content.

Relationship Building
We built relationships with industry influencers, bloggers, and publications, creating a network of natural linking opportunities.

Guest Content Strategy
We published on 15+ relevant industry publications, each providing quality backlinks and establishing thought leadership.

Authority Building
We listed the agency in key industry directories and resource pages, systematically building their backlink profile.
