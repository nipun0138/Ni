Client Profile

A fitness instructor with 6 months of YouTube content. Small but dedicated audience, but videos weren't discoverable. She was monetized but earning minimal ad revenue due to low view counts. Wanted to grow her channel and brand without paid promotion.

Channel Type: Fitness Content | Content Focus: Workout routines, fitness tips, transformations | Starting Monthly Views: 50K

The Challenge

Her videos had great content but weren't optimized for YouTube's search algorithm. Video titles were generic, descriptions lacked keywords, and thumbnails didn't stand out. Most views came from direct subscribers, not search or recommendations.

  • Generic video titles not targeting search keywords
  • Missing detailed descriptions with keyword targeting
  • No strategic use of tags and playlists
  • Thumbnails didn't encourage clicks
  • No SEO interconnection between videos
  • Limited understanding of YouTube algorithm optimization

YouTube SEO Strategy

Keyword Research for YouTube
We researched fitness keywords with high search volume and lower competition in YouTube. Identified keywords like "best 10 minute workout," "home exercises for beginners," "no equipment workout."

Video Optimization
We retooled her videos with keyword-rich titles, detailed descriptions explaining the workout and targeting search terms, and strategic tag implementation.

Thumbnail Strategy
We created higher-CTR thumbnails with contrasting colors, clear text overlays, and compelling facial expressions that stand out in search results and recommendations.

Playlist Organization
We created thematic playlists organizing videos by difficulty level and workout type. This improved watch time and helped YouTube understand content organization.

Channel SEO
We optimized channel description, added channel keywords, and created a consistent upload schedule that YouTube recommends.
