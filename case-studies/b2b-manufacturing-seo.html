Client Profile

A 50-year-old industrial equipment manufacturer with excellent products but minimal digital presence. Relied on trade shows and referrals for customer acquisition. Wanted to reach engineers and procurement managers searching for solutions online.

Business Type: B2B Manufacturing | Products: Specialized industrial equipment | Starting Leads: 8-12/month from organic

The Challenge

Industrial buyers research extensively before contacting suppliers. They search for technical specifications, application guides, and industry standards. The manufacturer's website was outdated with minimal technical content and no SEO optimization. Engineers found competitors' sites first.

  • No technical content for engineer and buyer research
  • Website didn't address buyer concerns or search queries
  • Competitors ranking for technical specification keywords
  • No thought leadership or authority positioning
  • Industry insights not shared publicly
  • Limited understanding of B2B buyer journey

B2B Manufacturing SEO Strategy

Technical Content Hub
We created a content hub with 30+ technical guides, application notes, and specification guides targeting engineer research keywords.

Thought Leadership Initiative
We positioned the company's engineering team as industry experts, publishing original research and white papers.

Buyer Journey Content
We created content for every stage: awareness (industry trends), consideration (comparison guides), and decision (ROI calculators, case studies).

Product Page Optimization
We optimized product pages with technical specifications, application scenarios, and buyer questions, targeting long-tail technical keywords.

Link Building Strategy
We earned backlinks from industry publications, engineering resources, and trade associations through quality content and outreach.
