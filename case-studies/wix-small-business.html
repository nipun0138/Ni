Client Profile

A solo pet sitting entrepreneur using Wix to build her business website. Offered pet sitting, dog walking, and pet care services in a competitive local market. Struggled with visibility despite being one of the most reliable pet sitters in the area.

Business: Pet Sitting Service | Platform: Wix | Service Area: Local metro area | Starting Monthly Customers: 5-7

The Challenge

Wix's SEO capabilities are often underestimated, and this business owner hadn't optimized her site at all. She ranked for no keywords and relied exclusively on referrals. Larger, well-funded pet care companies with dedicated marketing dominated local search results.

  • Zero keyword rankings despite having a live website
  • Google Business Profile not optimized
  • No local keyword targeting or location pages
  • Service pages not optimized for search
  • No reviews or social proof
  • Competing against established pet care brands

Wix SEO Strategy

Google Business & Review Strategy
We optimized her Google Business Profile and implemented a systematic review collection program. Encouraging happy clients to leave reviews significantly improved local rankings.

Local Keyword Optimization
We created location-specific service pages targeting keywords like "pet sitter near me," "dog walker [area]," and "pet care services [neighborhood]." Each page explained her services and experience.

Service Page Optimization
We rewrote service descriptions with keyword-rich content explaining pet sitting benefits, her experience, and client testimonials.

Wix SEO Setup
We configured Wix SEO settings properly, created a sitemap, and optimized metadata across all pages.

Content & Authority
We added a blog with pet care tips and pet safety content, positioning her as a knowledgeable pet professional.
