Client Profile

A mid-sized industrial equipment supplier operating a large Magento store with 8,000+ products across multiple categories. B2B focused, selling to contractors, manufacturers, and industrial buyers. Strong brand awareness but struggled with organic search visibility for their massive product catalog.

Platform: Magento | Products: 8,000+ | Starting Organic Revenue: $550K annually

The Challenge

Managing SEO for 8,000+ products requires strategic approach. Magento's default configurations don't optimize for search. Key challenges included crawl budget waste on thin pages, duplicate content issues, and lack of category page strategy for competitive B2B keywords.

  • Crawl budget wasted on parameter-heavy URLs and filter pages
  • Duplicate content from multiple category navigation paths
  • Thin product descriptions not optimized for keywords
  • Category pages using manufacturer descriptions without unique content
  • No internal linking strategy or content hub architecture
  • Poor technical implementation of product structured data

Magento E-Commerce Strategy

Technical SEO Foundation
We fixed crawl budget waste by implementing canonical tags properly, blocking filter pages from indexing, and optimizing robots.txt. We restructured URLs to be cleaner and more SEO-friendly.

Category Page Optimization
We created unique, keyword-rich descriptions for 60+ main categories. Each category page targeted broad B2B keywords while supporting product-level long-tail optimization.

Product Description Enhancement
We developed templates for product descriptions that naturally incorporated keywords, technical specifications, and use cases. Focus on B2B buyer needs and search intent.

Internal Linking Architecture
We created topic clusters and category hierarchies that connected related products and improved ranking distribution.

Content Hub Strategy
We created 40+ buying guides and technical resource pages targeting high-value B2B keywords and buyer journey stages.
