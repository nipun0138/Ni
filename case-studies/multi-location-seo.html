Client Profile

A fitness franchise operating 12 locations across a metropolitan area. Each location operated independently with minimal coordination. Corporate wanted to improve brand visibility and customer acquisition across all locations simultaneously.

Business: Fitness Franchise | Locations: 12 across metro area | Challenge: Coordinated multi-location SEO

The Challenge

Multi-location SEO is complex. Each location needs its own local optimization while maintaining brand consistency. Google Business Profiles need coordination, citations must be consistent across all locations, and content needs to scale across locations without being duplicated.

  • Inconsistent location information across platforms
  • Each location had weak individual authority
  • No coordinated content or keyword strategy
  • Locations competing with each other in search results
  • Budget constraints for managing 12 locations

Multi-Location SEO Strategy

Google Business Profile Coordination
We optimized and organized Google Business Profiles for all 12 locations with consistent branding while maintaining unique location-specific information.

Citation Standardization
We submitted all locations to 25+ local directories with completely consistent NAP information across all platforms.

Location Page Strategy
We created unique location pages on the corporate site with local keywords, class schedules, location-specific information, and unique content.

Review Generation Across Locations
We implemented location-specific review generation strategies, helping each location build its rating and review count.

Content Hub with Location Pages
We created scalable content templates that could be customized per location while maintaining core quality and consistency.
