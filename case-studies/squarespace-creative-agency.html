Client Profile

A 5-person creative design agency specializing in brand identity, web design, and print materials for small to mid-size businesses. Built on Squarespace, the agency had a beautiful portfolio but struggled to attract consistent inquiry flow. Most clients came through referrals, not search.

Business Type: Creative Services Agency | Platform: Squarespace | Services: Branding, Web Design, Print Design | Starting Monthly Inquiries: 6-8

The Challenge

While Squarespace provides beautiful templates and design flexibility, it has platform limitations for SEO. The agency's site ranked for branded searches only. They competed in a crowded "web design" market but weren't visible for specific, searchable queries potential clients used.

  • Limited control over technical SEO on Squarespace platform
  • Portfolio pages not optimized for search visibility
  • No service-specific landing pages with keyword targeting
  • Missing schema markup and structured data
  • No blog content to attract organic traffic
  • Competing against well-funded agencies with larger marketing budgets

Squarespace SEO Strategy

Platform Optimization Within Constraints
We maximized Squarespace's built-in SEO capabilities by optimizing page titles, meta descriptions, URL structures, and header hierarchy. We implemented proper schema markup for local business information.

Service-Specific Landing Pages
We created dedicated service pages targeting keywords like "brand identity design," "logo design company," and "web design for startups." Each page explained the design process, showcased relevant portfolio work, and included client testimonials.

Content Marketing Initiative
We launched a blog covering design trends, design process explanations, and case study narratives. Articles like "How to Choose a Web Designer" and "Brand Identity Design Process" attracted design-conscious prospects.

Portfolio Optimization
We optimized portfolio project descriptions with keyword-rich context. Each portfolio piece now included industry keywords, project challenges solved, and design approach explanations.

Local Authority Building
We built citations in design directories and local business listings. We earned backlinks from design blogs and creative industry publications.
