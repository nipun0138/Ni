Client Profile

A family-owned HVAC service company serving Denver, Colorado with 8 years of operating history. They provided excellent service but struggled with customer acquisition and relied heavily on referrals. Lead volume was unpredictable and competition from national HVAC chains with big marketing budgets was intense.

Service Area: Denver Metro | Services: AC repair, heating repair, HVAC maintenance, installation | Starting Monthly Leads: 8-12 qualified leads

The Challenge

The company's digital presence was minimal. They had outdated Google Business information, no review strategy, and minimal local citations. When customers searched for "HVAC repair near me" or "AC service Denver," competitors dominated local results while they were invisible.

  • Google Business Profile incomplete and outdated
  • No review management strategy
  • Missing local citations on major directories
  • Website had outdated content and poor mobile experience
  • No local keyword targeting or location pages
  • Competitors had extensive reviews (50-100+) while they had 5

Local SEO Strategy

Google Business Profile Optimization
We completely optimized their Google Business Profile with complete service area coverage, high-quality photos of their team and work, and detailed service descriptions. We updated it to appear modern and active.

Review Generation Program
We created a systematic approach to encourage customers to leave reviews. After completed service calls, they asked customers to review on Google. We provided easy-to-share links and incentives. Over 6 months, they accumulated 150+ reviews with an average 4.8/5 rating.

Local Citation Building
We submitted their business to 25+ local directories including Yelp, HomeAdvisor, Angie's List, Yellow Pages, Better Business Bureau, and niche HVAC directories. We ensured NAP consistency across all listings.

Local Content Strategy
We created location pages for their service areas (Denver, Aurora, Boulder, etc.) with unique content. We published 12 blog posts about HVAC maintenance, seasonal preparation, and energy-saving tips targeting local keywords.

Technical SEO
We improved website speed, mobile responsiveness, and implemented proper schema markup for local business information.
