Client Profile

A B2B SaaS platform providing project management solutions for remote teams. Founded 3 years prior, the company had found product-market fit but struggled to gain visibility among well-funded competitors. CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) through paid ads had become unsustainable.

Market: Project Management SaaS | Target: SMB to Mid-market | Starting Monthly MQL: 12-15 qualified leads

The Challenge

SaaS SEO is challenging due to intense competition from well-funded companies with massive marketing budgets. The client's website wasn't structured for search engines, lacked commercial intent content, and had minimal authority. Competitors ranked for all high-value keywords.

  • Weak domain authority compared to competitors with major funding
  • No clear topical authority in their specific niche
  • Minimal content targeting commercial/buying intent keywords
  • Website optimization focused on conversions, not search visibility
  • No backlink strategy or thought leadership positioning
  • Pricing and competitors dominated search results

B2B SaaS SEO Strategy

Content Authority Building
Rather than competing for broad commercial keywords immediately, we created authoritative content demonstrating deep expertise. We published 30+ comprehensive guides on remote team management, project workflow optimization, and team productivity—topics peripherally related to their software but valuable to their target market.

Commercial Intent Targeting
We created dedicated landing pages targeting high-intent keywords like "best project management software," "alternative to [competitor]," and "project management software comparison." These pages included detailed product comparisons, feature analysis, and migration guides.

Thought Leadership & Authority
We positioned the founder as a thought leader, publishing articles on remote work trends and team management challenges. This earned backlinks from industry publications and built domain authority faster.

Link Building Strategy
We earned 60+ backlinks from relevant B2B, SaaS, and business publications through strategic outreach and original research.

Product-Focused SEO
We optimized product pages with detailed feature explanations, use cases, and ROI messaging targeting searcher intent at different consideration stages.

B2B SaaS SEO Key Learnings

  • Content authority comes before commercial keyword rankings in SaaS SEO
  • Thought leadership content earns high-quality backlinks that boost authority
  • Comparison and alternative keywords convert better than generic product searches
  • Long content cycle (18+ months) is normal for competitive SaaS niches
  • Organic leads in B2B typically have 20-30% higher close rates than paid leads
  • CAC payback period improves dramatically as volume scales
