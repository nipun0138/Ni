Client Profile

A fintech startup offering investment and wealth management tools. Faced trust barriers in a competitive market. Realized that educational content could build authority and generate leads simultaneously.

Business: Fintech/Wealth Management | Starting Content Investment: $0 | Market Challenge: Trust and education barriers

The Challenge

People researching fintech solutions don't immediately convert. They need education, comparisons, and trust-building. The company needed a strategy to become the trusted educational authority while capturing long-term customer value.

  • High customer acquisition cost with paid ads
  • Complex product requiring education
  • Trust barriers in fintech industry
  • No organic content strategy
  • Competitors dominating educational content

Content Marketing Strategy

Educational Content Hub
We created comprehensive educational content: investment guides, financial planning articles, product comparison pieces, and wealth management resources.

SEO-Integrated Content
Every content piece targeted specific keywords their audience was searching for, integrated into a strategic keyword calendar.

Long-Form Authority Content
We created 30+ cornerstone pieces (5,000-8,000 words) establishing authority on key topics like "how to invest for retirement," "robo-advisor comparison," etc.

Content Distribution Strategy
We distributed content through multiple channels while optimizing for organic discovery as the primary channel.

Conversion Optimization
We tracked how content drove conversions and optimized conversion paths. Not all content was about direct sales - much was about building trust first.
