How Jannatul helps you

SEO work that turns analysis into practical action

Jannatul begins by auditing the current website to identify ranking blockers, missed content opportunities, and technical issues that weaken visibility. That audit is supported by competitor research and keyword planning so the strategy is based on actual search demand and realistic opportunities, not assumptions. From there, the work moves into on-page SEO improvements that strengthen page relevance, internal structure, metadata, and content direction for the pages that matter most.

Technical SEO is handled with the same practical approach, focusing on crawlability, indexing signals, site structure, and the foundations that help search engines interpret the website correctly. Performance tracking through Google Search Console and GA4 keeps the strategy grounded in evidence, while reporting stays clear enough to show what changed, why it mattered, and where the next opportunities are coming from.
