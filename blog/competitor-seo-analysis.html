Competitor SEO Analysis: Smarter Strategy

Learn how to reverse-engineer what's working and build a stronger SEO plan.

Published: July 2024 Reading time: 7 minutes

Why Competitor Analysis Matters

Understanding what your competitors are doing right helps you identify opportunities you might have missed. Competitor analysis shows you which strategies work in your industry and where gaps exist.

You're not copying competitors—you're learning from their successes and building a better strategy.

Keyword Analysis

Find keywords your competitors rank for using tools like Ahrefs or SEMrush. Look for keywords with decent volume that your competitors rank for but you don't. These are quick wins.

Analyze search intent for each keyword. Make sure the intent matches what you can offer.

Content Analysis

Review your competitors' top-performing content. What topics do they cover? How comprehensive are their articles? What angles do they take?

Create better versions of their content by being more comprehensive, more recent, or more useful.

Backlink Analysis

Analyze your competitors' backlink profiles. Where are they getting their authority? Can you earn links from those same sources?

Look for unlinked mentions of your competitors and reach out to claim those links for yourself.

Technical Analysis

Check your competitors' technical SEO implementation. What schema markup do they use? How fast do their sites load? Are they mobile-friendly?

If they're doing something well technically, implement it on your site too.

Conclusion

Competitor analysis provides valuable insights that inform your SEO strategy. Use it to identify opportunities and improve your competitive position.
